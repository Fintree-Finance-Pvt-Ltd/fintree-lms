CENTRAL KYC REGISTRY | Instructions / Checklist / Guidelines for filling Individual KYC Application Form

A. Clarification / Guidelines on filling the “Personal Details” section

  1. The name should match the name mentioned in the submitted Proof of Identity; otherwise the application is liable to be rejected.
  2. One of the following is mandatory: mother’s name, spouse’s name or father’s name.

B. Clarification / Guidelines on filling “Current Address details” section

  1. For overseas addresses, state/U.T. and pin/post code are not mandatory.
  2. Proof of address must be valid and current.
  3. Section 2, one of I, II or III must be selected. For online E-KYC authentication, II must be selected.
  4. Section 3, one of I, II, III, IV or V must be selected.
  5. List of accepted deemed proof-of-address documents includes recent utility bills, municipal/property tax receipts, pension orders containing address, employer allotment letters, and leave-and-licence agreements with eligible employers.
  6. Equivalent e-document means an electronic document issued by the relevant authority with a valid digital signature, including documents issued through Digital Locker.
  7. Digital KYC process must comply with applicable PML rules.
  8. “Offline verification” means verification carried out without authentication as prescribed.

C. Clarification / Guidelines on filling “Contact details” section

  1. Enter the two-digit country code and ten-digit mobile number, for example 91-9999999999.
  2. Do not add “0” at the beginning of the mobile number.

D. Clarification / Guidelines on filling “Related Person details” section

  1. Provide the KYC number of the related person, if available.

E. Clarification on Minor

  1. Guardian details are optional for minors above ten years of age for eligible account opening.
  2. Where applicable, guardian details and guardian KYC documentation must be provided.
